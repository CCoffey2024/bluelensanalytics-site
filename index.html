BlueLens Analytics is a cleared small business geospatial intelligence and AI firm operating in the NC Sandhills — the Fort Liberty corridor. We exist to bring senior, cleared, operationally-credentialed GEOINT and ML capability to prime contractors, IC agencies, and defense tech firms that need it without the overhead of a full-time hire.

For BD and contracting teams at the primes: BlueLens Analytics is available for subcontracting and teaming on existing and future NGA, SOCOM, and IC vehicles. The principal analyst holds a TS/SCI with polygraph, 30 years of domain expertise, and direct Project Maven credentials — the kind of CV that strengthens your technical proposal and wins task orders.

For defense tech firms: We provide senior geospatial AI consulting, computer vision system development, and production ML engineering on a project or retainer basis — without the friction of a permanent hire in a market where cleared senior talent is genuinely scarce.
